How AXION Works

A multi-agent AI pipeline from article to animation

01
Scriptwriter

GPT-5 reads your article and generates a structured storyboard with narration and visual descriptions for each scene.

GPT-5
02
Voiceover

Google Cloud TTS synthesizes natural-sounding speech for each scene in your chosen language.

Google TTS
03
Animator

GPT-5.1-Codex generates Manim Python code, rendered in a Docker sandbox with self-correction on errors.

Manim CE
04
Composer

FFMPEG merges audio and animation per scene, burns multilingual subtitles, and outputs the final MP4.
